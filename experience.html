Cultural Night: 'Embrace, Engage, Enlight

UiTM Dengkil

2019

During my studies in UiTM Dengkil, I was actively involve in Performing Arts, Creative Writing and Critique Club activities. One of the big event I had joined as a performer is PACC Cultural Night event. It was really hectic during the preparation since we only have two weeks for the practice and all preparation. It is really exhausted but satisfying because we manage to do the best performance and engage well with the audience. The event is held at Studio Orkid and it is full house! I am really happy and enjoy that moments and experience. It was the first time I did my performance after six years not involves in any cultural activites. I performed as the traditional malay dancer in that Cultural Night. We also performed ayam didik and that is the best part of our performance.
