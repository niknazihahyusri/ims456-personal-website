Cultural Night: 'Embrace, Engage, Enlight

UiTM Dengkil

2019

During my studies in UiTM Dengkil, I was actively involve in Performing Arts, Creative Writing and Critique Club activities. One of the big event I had joined as a performer is PACC Cultural Night event. It was really hectic during the preparation since we only have two weeks for the practice and all preparation. It is really exhausted but satisfying because we manage to do the best performance and engage well with the audience. The event is held at Studio Orkid and it is full house! I am really happy and enjoy that moments and experience. It was the first time I did my performance after six years not involves in any cultural activites. I performed as the traditional malay dancer in that Cultural Night. We also performed ayam didik and that is the best part of our performance. Kursus Pengurusan Organisasi, KPO

UiTM Machang

2022

Kursus Pengurusan Organisasi is held in the early of this semester, which is my 4th semester. It was really great to get the opportunity to gather with High Committee student represented from their club. I think it was the best experience during my degree time. A lot of activities had been organised by Unit Kepimpinan UiTM Machang regarding the management of club activities and event. We also have adventurous activities which is hiking Bukit Keluang and Kem Wawasan (Ladang Nanas UiTM Machang). Overall, I was happy and exhausted at the same time during the programme but it was fun since it was the first physical event after a year we faced the pandemic. So, I am really grateful for that opportunity.
